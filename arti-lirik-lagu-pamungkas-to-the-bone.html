17. [tex]quiz \: night.........[/tex]Apa arti kata "To the bone" ?​


Definisi idiom to the bone adalah
1. Very much
Sangat banyak/sungguh
Example: we were frozen to the bone
Kami sungguh kedinginan
2. As much as possible
Sebanyak mungkin
Example: the company has cut costs to the bone in an effort to save money
Perusahaan telah memotong biaya sebanyak mungkin dalam upaya untuk menghemat uang
