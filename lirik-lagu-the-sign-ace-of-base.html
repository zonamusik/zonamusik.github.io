13. The area of the base of cube is 81 cm. Find the volume of the cube. bantu bg lagu uas ​


Jawaban:

729 cm³

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

The area of the base is 81 cm² which equals to s²

s² = 81

s = 9 cm

The volume of cube is s³ = 9³ = 729 cm³

s = √v
