9. the acid of normal rain is....then the acid rain​


Jawaban:

B. lower

Penjelasan:

jadikan jawaban tercerdas ya

Jawaban:

B. Lower

Penjelasan:

"The acid of normal rain is . . . than the acid rain." berarti "Tingkat keasaman hujan normal . . . dibanding dengan hujan asam."

Berdasarkan kalimat "Acid rain is rain that is highly acidic because of sulphur oxides, nitrogen oxides, and other air pollutants dissolved in it. Normal rain is slightly acidic." (Hujan asam adalah hujan yang sangat asam karena sulfur oksida, nitrogen oksida, dan polutan udara lainnya terlarut di dalamnya. Hujan normal sedikit asam), maka dapat disimpulkan bahwa hujan normal memiliki tingkat keasaman yang lebih rendah (lower) dibandingkan dengan hujan asam.

Jadi, jawaban yang benar adalah B.

14. script singkat rapunzel dalam bahasa inggris beserta terjemahannya


RAPUNZEL

Rapunzel was the most beautiful child in the world. When she was twelve years old the witch shut her up in a tower in the midst of a wood, and it had neither steps nor door, only a small window above. After they had lived thus a few years it happened that as the Prince was riding through the wood, he came to the tower; and as he drew near he heard a voice singing so sweetly that he stood still and listened. It was Rapunzel in her loneliness trying to pass away the time with sweet songs. The Prince wished to go in to her, and sought to find a door in the tower, but there was none. So he rode home, but the song had entered into his heart, and every day he went into the wood and listened to it. Once, as he was standing under a tree, he saw the witch come up, and listened while she called out," O Rapunzel, Rapunzel! let down your hair."


Then he saw how Rapunzel let down her long tresses, and how the witch climbed up by it and went in to her, and he said to himself, "Since that is the ladder I will climb it, and seek my fortune." And the next day, as soon as it began to grow dusk, he went in to the tower and cried, "O Rapunzel, Rapunzel! let down your hair."


And she let down her hair, and the King’s son climbed up by it. Rapunzel was greatly terrified when she saw that a man had come in to her, for she had never seen one before; but the Prince began speaking so kindly to her. Then Rapunzel forgot her terror, and when he asked her to take him for her husband, she said yes.


One day, the witch knew everything and she became very angry with Rapunzel. She cut her hair and put her in a desert place. Then the witch came back to the tower. In the night the prince came and cried, “O Rapunzel, Rapunzel! Let down your hair!”


Then she let her hair down, and the prince climbed up, but there was no Rapunzel in the tower. There was the witch who was very angry with him. The prince tried to go down but he fell. He became blind. He wandered several years until at last he came to the desert place where Rapunzel lived. When Rapunzel saw him, she hugged him and cried. And when her tears touched his eyes the became clear again and he could see her again. Then he took her to his kingdom, and there lived long and happily.


Terjemahan:

Rapunzel adalah anak yang paling cantik di dunia. Saat dia berumur 12 tahun, penyihir mengurungnya di menara di dalam hutan. Menara itu tidak memiliki tangga maupun pintu; hanya ada sebuah jendela kecil di atas. Suatu hari, pangeran sedang berkuda melewati hutan. Dia melihat sebuah menara dan berjalan mendekat ke menara tersebut. Saat dia mendekat, dia mendengar sebuah suara yang sedang menyanyi dengan indah sehingga dia terpaku dan mendengarkannya. Itu adalah Rapunzel yang kesepian berusaha untuk melewati waktu dengan menyanyikan lagu-lagu yang indah. Pangeran ingin bertemu dengannya, dan berusaha mencari pintu masuk ke dalam menara, tapi tidak ada. Lalu, saat ia sedang berdiri di bawah pohon, dia melihat penyihir yang datang, dan mendengarnya saat penyihir memanggil, "Oh, Rapunzel! Oh, Rapunzel! Turunkan rambutmu."


Kemudian, dia melihat bagaimana Rapunzel menurunkan rambutnya, dan bagaimana penyihir mendaki dengan rambut itu lalu masuk menemui Rapunzel, dan ia berkata pada dirinya sendiri, "Karena itu adalah tangganya maka aku akan mendakinya dan mencari keberuntunganku." Dan esok harinya, di sore hari ia pergi ke menara dan berteriak, "Oh, Rapunzel! Oh, Rapunzel! Turunkan rambutmu."


Dan Rapunzel pun menurunkan rambutnya, dan pangeran mendaki menara dengan rambut itu. Rapunzel sangat ketakutan saat ia melihat seorang pria telah mendatanginya, karena dia tidak pernah melihat seorang pria sebelumnya; tapi pangeran berkata dengan baik kepadanya. Kemudian Rapunzel telah melupakan ketakutannya, dan saat pangeran meminta untuk menikah dengannya, ia berkata ya.


Suatu hari, penyihir mengetahui segalanya dan ia menjadi sangat marah terhadap Rapunzel. Dia memotong rambutnya dan meletakkannya di tempat terpencil. Kemudian, penyihir kembali ke menara. Di malam hari, pangeran datang dan berteriak, "Oh, Rapunzel! Oh, Rapunzel! Turunkan rambutmu."


Kemudian, ia menurunkan rambutnya, dan pangeran mendakinya, tapi tidak ada Rapunzel di menara. Yang ada ialah penyihir yang sangat marah terhadapnya. Pangeran berusaha untuk turun, tapi ia terjatuh. Dia menjadi buta. Dia mengembara selama beberapa tahun sampai akhirnya ia sampai ke tempat terpencil dimana Rapunzel tinggal. Saat Rapunzel melihatnya, ia memeluknya dan menangis. Dan saat air matanya mengenai mata pangeran, ia bisa melihat kembali dan ia bisa melihat Rapunzel lagi. Kemudian, ia membawanya ke kerajaannya dan mereka hidup bahagia selamanya.
