Justin Drew Bieber (/ˈbiːbər/; born March 1, 1994) is a Canadian singer and songwriter. After a talent manager discovered him through his YouTube videos covering songs in 2008 and signed to RBMG, Bieber released his debut EP, My World, in late 2009. It was certified Platinum in the U.S. He became the first artist to have seven songs from a debut record chart on the Billboard Hot 100. Bieber released his first full-length studio album, My World 2.0, in 2010. It debuted at or near number one in several countries, was certified triple Platinum in the U.S., and contained his single “Baby”.

Following his debut album, he had his first headlining tour, the My World Tour, released the remix albums My Worlds Acoustic and Never Say Never – The Remixes—and the 3D biopic-concert film Justin Bieber: Never Say Never. He released his second studio album, Under the Mistletoe, in November 2011, when it debuted at number one on the Billboard 200. Bieber released his third studio album, Believe, in 2012. His fourth studio album Purpose was released in November 2015, spawning three number one singles: “What Do You Mean?”, “Sorry”, and “Love Yourself”. Following the release of Purpose, Bieber was featured on several successful collaborations, including “Cold Water”, “Let Me Love You”, the remix of “Despacito” and “I’m the One”. His U.S. album and singles sales total 44.7 million. He has sold an estimated 100 million records, making him one of the world’s best-selling music artists.

Bieber has won numerous awards, including the American Music Award for Artist of the Year in 2010 and 2012. In his career, he has won one Grammy Award for Best Dance Recording for the song “Where Are Ü Now” at the 2016 ceremony. He has been listed four times by Forbes magazine among the top ten most powerful celebrities in the world in 2011, 2012, and 2013. Bieber also became the first artist to surpass 10 billion total video views on Vevo.

25. Temukan Pharasal verb pada lagu Ghost Justin Bieber !


Jawaban:

Beberapa phrasal verbs pada lagu "Ghost" Justin Bieber antara lain:

1. "Fall apart" : mengacu pada perasaan putus asa atau hancur dalam hubungan yang telah berakhir.

Contoh dalam lirik lagu : "I tried to give you everything, but you fall apart"

2. "Runaway" : artinya kabur, dalam lagu ini mengacu pada ingin pergi dari situasi sulit atau hubungan yang buruk.

Contoh dalam lirik lagu : "Can't choose when you made your mind up, you're gonna runaway"

3. "Let go": artinya melepaskan, dalam lagu ini mengacu pada perasaan sulit untuk melepaskan seseorang di masa lalu.

Contoh dalam lirik lagu : "I just wish I could let go of us"

4. "Hold on" : artinya bertahan, dalam lagu ini mengacu pada seseorang yang kesulitan untuk melepaskan keinginan untuk kembali bersama.

Contoh dalam lirik lagu : "Holding on to things that we said"

5. "Turn around" : artinya berbalik, dalam lagu ini mengacu pada keinginan untuk membuat seseorang berbalik dan kembali pada hubungan yang telah berakhir.

Contoh dalam lirik lagu : "I know you're thinking you can turn around, but I'm begging please"

Justin Drew Bieber (born March 1, 1994)is a Canadian singer and songwriter. After a talent manager discovered him through his YouTube videos covering songs in 2008 and signed to RBMG, Bieber released his debut EP, My World, in late 2009. It was certified Platinum in the U.S.He became the first artist to have seven songs from a debut record chart on the Billboard Hot 100. Bieber released his first full-length studio album, My World 2.0, in 2010. It debuted at or near number one in several countries and was certified triple Platinum in the U.S.It was preceded by his most successful single to date, "Baby".

Following his debut album, he had his first headlining tour, the My World Tour, released the remix albums My Worlds Acoustic and Never Say Never – The Remixes—and the 3D biopic-concert film Justin Bieber: Never Say Never. He released his second studio album, Under the Mistletoe, in November 2011, when it debuted at number one on the Billboard 200. Bieber released his third studio album, Believe, in 2012. His fourth studio album Purpose was released in November 2015. His U.S. album and singles sales total 44.7 million. He has sold an estimated 100 million records, making him one of the world’s best-selling music artists.

Bieber has won numerous fan-voted awards, including American Music Award for Artist of the Year in 2010 and 2012. In his career, he has received three Grammy nominations, winning one for Best Dance Recording for the song "Where Are Ü Now" at the 2016 ceremony. His fan base, dubbed "beliebers", largely consists of early to mid-adolescent girls. He has been listed four times by Forbes magazine among the top ten most powerful celebrities in the world in 2011, 2012, and 2013.Bieber also became the first artist to surpass 10 billion total video views on Vevo.

40. cara mengolah napas pada saat bernyanyi justin bieber dan agnes monica


Bernapaslah menggunakan diafragma. Saat menyanyi, pastikan Anda bernafas dalam-dalam menggunakan diafragma atau otot perut. Dengan demikian, Anda tidak menyimpan terlalu banyak udara di tenggorok yang membuat suara menjadi tegang. Lakukan langkah berikut untuk memastikan bahwa Anda sedang bernapas menggunakan diafragma.Berdirilah tegak sambil memegang sisi luar pinggang (antara tulang panggul dan tulang rusuk paling bawah). Kemudian, tarik napas dalam-dalam sampai jari tangan saling menjauh.Cara lain, berbaringlah telentang di lantai lalu tarik napas dalam-dalam sampai otot perut mengembang, tetapi dada tidak ikut mengembang.Langkah di atas membantu Anda mengetahui seperti apa rasanya bernapas menggunakan diafragma.
