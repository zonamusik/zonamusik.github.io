My friend

I have a friend and his name is Adi. One day, we went to a swimming pool together. We swam together in the shallow pool for kids. We competed to find a coin in the bottom of the shallow pool. It was so much fun. After a while, I asked Adi to swim in the deeper pool. The deeper pool is 2.5 meters deep. To my surprise, Adi refused it because he couldn't swim well. Adi was afraid of drowning.

I kept asking Adi to swim with me in the deeper pool and I assured him that he would be fine swimming by my side.
