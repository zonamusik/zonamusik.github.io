6. Lirik lagu "Nothing's gonna change my love for you" past tense atau present tense


Lirik lagu "Nothing's gonna change my love for you" menggunakan present tense karena menggambarkan perasaan cinta yang tetap berlangsung di masa sekarang. Pada lirik tersebut, penggunaan "gonna" adalah singkatan dari "going to" yang mengindikasikan tindakan atau keadaan di masa depan yang pasti terjadi.

Misalnya, frasa "Nothing's gonna change my love for you" berarti bahwa tidak ada yang akan mengubah cinta seseorang dalam waktu sekarang atau di masa depan. Dalam konteks lirik lagu ini, perasaan cinta yang dipaparkan oleh penyanyi masih berlaku dan berlanjut di saat ini, tidak peduli apa yang terjadi.

1. Introduction/Instrumental: The song may begin with an instrumental introduction that sets the mood and captures the listener's attention.

2. Verse 1: The first verse introduces the main theme or story of the song. It presents the initial lyrics and melody that lead into the chorus.

3. Chorus: The chorus is a section of the song that usually has a catchy and memorable melody. It often contains the main hook or the central message of the song. The chorus is repeated throughout the song, typically after each verse.

4. Verse 2: The second verse continues to develop the story or theme introduced in the first verse. It may contain new lyrics or expand upon the ideas presented in the chorus.

5. Chorus: The chorus is repeated again after the second verse, reinforcing the main message of the song.

6. Bridge: The bridge is a section that provides contrast to the rest of the song. It often has a different melody, lyrics, or musical arrangement. The bridge adds variety and serves as a transition between different parts of the song.

7. Chorus: After the bridge, the chorus is repeated once again, providing familiarity and reinforcing the main hook of the song.

8. Outro: The outro is the final section of the song, serving as a conclusion or a fade-out. It may include a repetition of the chorus or a variation of the instrumental section from the introduction.

It's important to note that song structures can vary, and different songs may have additional sections such as pre-chorus, post-chorus, or additional verses. The structure of a song ultimately depends on the creativity and artistic choices of the songwriter or composer.
