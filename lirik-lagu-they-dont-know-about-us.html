10. Help me pleasee about me dont know that answer..[tex]8[/tex]​


Jawaban:

rumus pertama maksudnya subjek/nama orang di tambah have /punya kemudian objek / benda

Jawaban:

summary:

penggunaan VERB (KATA KERJA).

Kata kerja biasanya menggunakan Has / Have.

Has digunakan untuk to be:

1. He = menjadi He has + / He does not have - / Does he have ?

2. She = menjadi She has + / She does not have - / Does she have ?

3. It = menjadi It has + / It does not have - / Does it have ?

Have digunakan untuk to be:

1. They = menjadi They have + / They do not have - / Do They have ?

2. We = menjadi We have + / We do not have - / Do they have ?

3. I = menjadi I have + / I do not have - / Do I have ?

4. You = menjadi You have + / You do not have - / Do You have ?
