Dalam obrolan internet 2001 dengan para penggemar, Jackson mengatakan bahwa "Heal the World" adalah lagu yang paling ia banggakan. Dia juga menciptakan Heal the World Foundation, sebuah organisasi amal yang dirancang untuk meningkatkan kehidupan anak - anak. Organisasi itu juga dimaksudkan untuk mengajar anak - anak bagaimana membantu orang lain. Konsep "perbaikan untuk semua" ini akan menjadi pusat perhatian untuk Dangerous World Tour. Dalam film dokumenter Living with Michael Jackson, Jackson mengatakan ia menciptakan lagu dalam "Giving Tree" di Neverland Ranch.

11. heal the world song expresses the writer's​


Understanding the Context from the Song

Heal the World song expresses the writer's concern about the world's condition. This song delivers a message that ​everyone can change the world no matter what you are and what you do. People can all do something to make the world better. For example, helping the poor people, keeping the environment, making the world more peaceful and happy and so on.

"There's a place in your heart" is an example of metaphor in the lyrics as heart is compared to a place. This helps connects the message of the song by saying that everyone has a heart for helping other people. It says that we should treat each other equally and stop the discrimination. This song shows how powerful one gesture of kindness really is and how someone can change the world and make it an enjoyable and loving place for everyone across the world.

Terjemahannya adalah sebagai berikut:

Lagu Heal the World mengekspresikan kekhawatiran penulis tentang kondisi dunia. Lagu ini menyampaikan pesan penulis bahwa setiap orang dapat mengubah dunia tidak peduli siapa pun kamu dan apa pun  pekerjaanmu. Semua orang dapat melakukan sesuatu untuk membuat dunia lebih baik. Misalnya, membantu orang miskin, menjaga lingkungan, membuat dunia lebih damai dan bahagia dan sebagainya.

"Ada tempat di hatimu" adalah contoh metafora dalam lirik karena hati dibandingkan dengan tempat. Ini membantu menghubungkan pesan lagu dengan mengatakan bahwa setiap orang memiliki hati untuk membantu orang lain. Dikatakan bahwa kita harus memperlakukan satu sama lain dengan setara dan menghentikan diskriminasi. Lagu ini menunjukkan betapa kuatnya satu gerakan kebaikan dan bagaimana seseorang dapat mengubah dunia dan menjadikannya tempat yang menyenangkan dan penuh kasih bagi semua orang di seluruh dunia.

27. what parts of the song heal the world


The song 'Heal The World' by Michael Jackson is one of the timeless songs about humanity. It has been and will always be played for promoting the act of keeping the world peace along with John Lennon's 'Imagine' or Black eyed Peas' 'Where is the love?'.

The question in the exercise seems to be incomplete or unfinished. Yet, it might be concluded that the focus of the query is about the part of the lyrics which tells about the main theme.  

Penjelasan:

The part of song in the lyric which can obviously tell about the main theme, which is to make a better world or promote peace around the world is in the refrain part.

Heal the world / make it a better place for you and for me and the entire of human race.There are people dying / if you care enough for a livingMake a better place for you and for me.

From the part, one can see that all we need is to care for each other to make our world a better place for all of us, not just for one side only.

The other part of the song which tells about the theme can be seen in the one of the stanzas in the middle.

the why do we keep strangling lifewound this earth / crucify its soul though it's plain to seethis world is heavenly/ be God's glow.

This part tells about the disappointment towards human actions to make war, try to rule the world without caring for the environment. The idea of world domination, in whatever form, sacrifice people more than anything, would make the world ruin.
