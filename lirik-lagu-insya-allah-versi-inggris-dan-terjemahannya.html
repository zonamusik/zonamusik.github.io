3. Lagu Maher Zein, insya Allah Bahasa inggris, cari liriknya yang ada kalimat adjective, Subject, dan apa pesan dari lagu itu? Catatan : Jawab Pakai Bahasa Inggris


Answer: 

In this popular pop religious song by Swedish artist, Maher Zein , some of the adjective sentences (the sentence which has adjectives in it) are
- You're so confused
- It's way too late
- He's never far away
- you're so alone

Meanwhile, the subject of this song is us the listener and all Moslem in the world who are familiar with it. This song is more like an encouragement for us to have faith in Allah, no matter what. 
The message in this song is that we should keep praying and believing in Allah's blessing despite the misfortune, or feeling despair as Allah the Almighty God would listen your prayers and grant your wish whenever it's considered proper in time.

12. Fungsi sosial lirik lagu dalam bahasa inggris dan terjemahannya


The song is one product of a music. However, it is not merely for fun. Song has other function too apart from entertaining people. Such is called social function.    

Penjelasan:

The social function of the song is the function which can lead people to do something to their society or at least other people around them. Here are the social function of a song:

to inspire people to do somethingto criticise certain parties like government or tyrant to be the voice of common people

(Terjemahan:
