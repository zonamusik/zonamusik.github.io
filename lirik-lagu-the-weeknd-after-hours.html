22. the number of bacteria in a colony is 2^t+1, where t is the number of hours after the first observation. after how many hours will there be 1024 bacteria?​


Jawab:

9,9 or if we round it is around 10 hours

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Let say that the expansion equation is written down as:

f(x) = 2^t + 1, where f(x) is the amount of bacteria observed. Therefore, we can finish the question by:

f(x) = 2^t + 1

1024 = 2^t + 1

1023 = 2^t

Since we know that:

a^x = b ⇒ log(a)b = x, then:

t = log(2)1023

t = 9,9 or if we round it is around 10 hours
