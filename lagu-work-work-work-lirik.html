6. KUIS5! NT= WORK WORK MUDAH​

= 5x4x3x2x1

[tex] \\ [/tex]

Jawaban:

5! = 120

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

5!

= 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1

= (5 + 5 + 5 + 5) × 3 × 2 × 1

= (10 + 5 + 5) × 3 × 2 × 1

= (15 + 5) × 3 × 2 × 1

= 20 × 3 × 2 × 1

= (20 + 20 + 20) × 2 × 1

= (40 + 20) × 2 × 1

= 60 × 2 × 1

= (60 + 60) × 1

= 120 × 1

= 120 ✔
